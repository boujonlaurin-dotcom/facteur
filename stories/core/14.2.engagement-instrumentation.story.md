### Mobile — Call-sites

- `features/digest/screens/digest_screen.dart` — fires `digest_opened` + `digest_item_viewed` once per digest, via `ref.listen(digestProvider, …)`. Dedup via `_trackedDigestId`.
- `features/feed/widgets/perspectives_bottom_sheet.dart` — `initState` → opened, per-card `onTap` → article viewed (unique), `dispose` → closed with elapsed seconds + viewed count.
- `features/digest/widgets/article_thumbs_feedback.dart` — mirrors thumbs submission (positive → `thumbs_up`, negative → `thumbs_down` with reasons/comment flags). `origin: 'digest'`.
- `features/feed/screens/feed_screen.dart` — emits `article_feedback_submitted` on the three inline dismiss reasons (`less_source`, `less_topic`, `already_seen`). `origin: 'feed'`.
- `features/onboarding/screens/questions/subtopics_question.dart` — fires `suggestion_shown` once per unique subtopic across selected themes (post-frame), `added`/`removed` on toggle. `origin: 'onboarding'`.
- `features/custom_topics/providers/custom_topics_provider.dart` — wires `added`/`removed` at provider level (covers all UI surfaces: topic explorer, topic row swipe, topic chip sheet). `origin: 'custom_topics'`.
- `features/settings/providers/theme_provider.dart` — `preference_changed` on `setThemeMode`.
- `features/settings/screens/notifications_screen.dart` — `preference_changed` on push toggle (fired optimistically at tap, regardless of OS permission outcome).
